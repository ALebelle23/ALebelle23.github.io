My Process

My current process always starts with choosing a color palette for my designs. After that, I prepare a layout outline either on paper or using a service such as Figma. Then, I try to find all the images I will need to include on the site. Finally, I'll start to prepare the site based off the outline. I've found NN/g to be an important source of information throughout making my sample designs.
